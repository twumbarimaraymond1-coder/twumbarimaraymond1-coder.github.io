About me

Raymond Twum-Barima

MEng Mechanical Engineering student at Loughborough University — thermofluids, computational engineering and software.

Overview

Final-year MEng Mechanical Engineering student at Loughborough University, completing the degree after a year in industry at the UK Atomic Energy Authority. Focus areas: thermofluids and computational engineering — finite element analysis, CFD, numerical methods, and applied machine learning.

At UKAEA: structural and thermal FEA of cryogenic components for fusion research, including modelling thermal contraction at −196 °C and correlating simulation results with physical tests. See the projects page for detail.

Activities & interests

  • Founder and Chair, Loughborough University Catholic Society (2023–24, 2025–26).
  • Former Head Boy, Bishop Challoner Catholic Federation of Schools.
  • Runner (Strava) and choir member.
  • Interest in history and historical strategy games.

Availability

Open to graduate and summer engineering roles, particularly in thermofluids, FEA or simulation.
